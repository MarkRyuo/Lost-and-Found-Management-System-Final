Lost and Found Management System

About

Welcome to Lost and Found Management System, We are here to return the items to the rightful owners.

The Lost in Found Management System is a comprehensive and efficient platform dedicated to the meticulous organization and retrieval of lost items within diverse settings.

Our system operates on the principle of streamlining the process of reuniting lost possessions with their rightful owners by employing advanced technologies and user-friendly interfaces.

The Steps Student Will Do

The first step you will do is to login your Srcode which is the account at your school.

Then, the second step once you login in our system, if you find your missing item on our website, you may proceed to the Security Guard directly.

And the last step, Once you're with the security guard, you need to prove that the items is yours.

The Security Guard Will Do

If you don't have account in our system you may go to the admin which is the CICS department.

Once you have account you need to login it then you may proceed to the our System.

When a student comes in and reports a lost item, simply click on report missing and fill in the blanks with what is gone.

Then, After you report the item you need to go to the viewlost item to see if the item you reported has entered.

When the student has proved that the item belongs to him, you have to go to the claim report missing, then click "Claim" the item when its got.
